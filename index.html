About Me

Olen is a full stack web developer with a focus on scalable applications that take advantage of industry standard frameworks. Recently, Olen enrolled in Georgia Tech's Bootcamp in order to hone his self-taught web development skills and take his career to the next level.

On the rare occasions that Olen is not coding, he enjoys the following activities, in no particular order:

  • Riding around Atlanta and other locations on various and sundry bicycles;
  • Photography with vintage film cameras; and
  • Relaxing on the couch with an extremly large cat for companionship.
